Labrador Retriever

The Labrador, or Lab is a medium-large breed of retriever-gun dog. The Labrador is the most popular breed of dog in many countries around the world.The breed is best known for their obedience, loyalty, and playful composure. Additionally, they are prized as sporting and hunting dogs. Labrador Retrievers are registered in three colours: black (a solid black colour), yellow (considered from cream to fox-red), and chocolate (medium to dark brown).

German Sheperd

The German Shepherd is a breed of medium to large-sized working dog that originated in Germany. As a herding dog, German Shepherds are working dogs developed originally for herding sheep. They have a double coat which is close and dense with a thick undercoat. The coat is accepted in two variants; medium and long. The long-hair gene is recessive, making the long-hair variety rarer. Most commonly, They are either tan/black or red/black.
